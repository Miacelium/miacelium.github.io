How to disable Firefox DRM message

In order to disable Mozilla Firefox from showing: You must enable DRM to play some audio or video on this page. Learn more followed by a button to enable DRM in the Firefox settings,
you need to got into the about:config and look for media.gmp-widevinecdm.enabled and media.gmp-widevinecdm.visible and set both to false. Once you've done that, the notification shouldn't appear again.

Tested in Firefox 110.0 (64-bit).
